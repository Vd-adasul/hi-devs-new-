STATEMENT OF WORK #03

This Statement of Work ("SOW") is entered into as of November 18, 2025 ("Effective Date") and is governed by the Master Services Agreement dated June 30, 2025 between Demo Org, Inc. ("Provider") and Ironbridge Industrial Group ("Customer"). In the event of any conflict between this SOW and the MSA, the MSA shall control.

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
1.1 Name. "Project Forge — Supply-Chain ERP Integration."
1.2 Duration. December 1, 2025 through January 30, 2026 (nine weeks).
1.3 Objective. Integrate Customer's SAP ERP with three regional supplier portals. Replace manual purchase-order workflows with EDI-based real-time exchange.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider shall perform the following services:
(a) Discovery and EDI mapping across three supplier portals (Week 1–2);
(b) SAP integration layer build (Week 2–5);
(c) EDI X12 transaction set implementation (850, 855, 856, 810) (Week 4–6);
(d) UAT with two pilot suppliers, then rollout to all three (Week 6–8);
(e) Documentation, training, and handoff (Week 9).

3. MILESTONES AND DELIVERABLES

Milestone 1 — EDI Mapping Complete                Due: Dec 12, 2025   Fee: $26,000
  Deliverables:
    - EDI mapping spec for all 4 transaction sets
    - SAP iDoc-to-EDI translation table
    - Supplier conformance test plan

Milestone 2 — Integration Layer in QA             Due: Jan 9, 2026    Fee: $38,000
  Deliverables:
    - SAP middleware deployed to non-prod
    - All 4 X12 transactions round-tripping cleanly
    - Error queue + retry mechanism implemented

Milestone 3 — Pilot Suppliers Live in Production  Due: Jan 23, 2026   Fee: $40,000
  Deliverables:
    - Two suppliers exchanging POs end-to-end in prod
    - 30-day soak metrics meeting SLAs
    - Cutover rollback runbook validated

Milestone 4 — Full Rollout + Handoff              Due: Jan 30, 2026   Fee: $38,000
  Deliverables:
    - Third supplier live and stable
    - Operational handoff document
    - Two training sessions delivered (procurement + IT ops)

TOTAL FIXED FEE: $142,000 (exclusive of pre-approved EDI VAN charges)
