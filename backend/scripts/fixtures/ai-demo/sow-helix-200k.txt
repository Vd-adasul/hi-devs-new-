STATEMENT OF WORK — DRAFT FOR CUSTOMER REVIEW

This Statement of Work ("SOW") would, if executed, be entered into between Helix Systems, LLC ("Provider") and Demo Org, Inc. ("Customer"), under a Master Services Agreement to be negotiated separately. This document is a quote and proposal; it does not bind either Party until both have signed.

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
1.1 Name. "Project Lattice — Multi-Region Streaming Data Platform."
1.2 Proposed Duration. June 1, 2026 through July 24, 2026 (eight weeks).
1.3 Objective. Stand up a multi-region streaming data platform for Customer's customer-event telemetry. Migrate the existing nightly Snowflake batch jobs to real-time ingestion. Deliver a complete operational handoff.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider shall perform the following services:
(a) Architecture and capacity-planning workshop (Week 1);
(b) Multi-region streaming ingestion build (Week 1–4);
(c) Migration of fourteen (14) batch jobs to streaming (Week 3–5);
(d) Load test, failover test, and chaos validation (Week 6);
(e) Documentation, training, and go-live support (Week 7–8).

3. MILESTONES AND DELIVERABLES

Milestone 1 — Architecture Sign-Off               Due: Jun 8, 2026    Fee: $35,000
  Deliverables:
    - Reference architecture diagrams
    - Capacity model with cost projections
    - Disaster-recovery plan

Milestone 2 — Region 1 Production                 Due: Jun 26, 2026   Fee: $55,000
  Deliverables:
    - Streaming ingestion deployed to us-east-1
    - Schema registry populated, observability live
    - SLOs defined and monitored

Milestone 3 — Cross-Region + Migrations           Due: Jul 10, 2026   Fee: $60,000
  Deliverables:
    - Region 2 live (us-west-2) with auto-failover

Milestone 4 — Validation + Handoff                Due: Jul 24, 2026   Fee: $50,000
  Deliverables:
