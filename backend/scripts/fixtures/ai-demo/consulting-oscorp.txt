CONSULTING AGREEMENT

This Consulting Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of August 20, 2026 ("Effective Date") by and between Demo Org, Inc. ("Client") and OsCorp Industries LLC ("Consultant").

1. SERVICES
Consultant shall provide the services described in SOW #1 attached hereto, which concerns strategic advisory on the expansion of Client's European operations. Additional SOWs may be added by mutual signed agreement.

2. COMPENSATION
2.1 Hourly Rate. Consultant shall be compensated at $400 per hour for principal time and $225 per hour for senior associate time.
2.2 Not-to-Exceed. Total fees under SOW #1 shall not exceed SEVENTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS ($75,000) without Client's prior written approval.
2.3 Expenses. Pre-approved, reasonable travel expenses (economy airfare, mid-tier hotels, standard meals) shall be reimbursed at cost with receipts.
2.4 Invoicing. Monthly, itemized with time entries in 6-minute increments. Net 30.

3. TERM
3.1 Initial Term: six (6) months from the Effective Date.
3.2 Extension by mutual written agreement.
3.3 Early termination: either Party may terminate for any reason on thirty (30) days written notice. Fees earned through the termination date are payable.

4. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR
Consultant is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, or partner of Client. Consultant is responsible for its own taxes, insurance, and benefits. No authority to bind Client.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Consultant shall protect Client's confidential information for five (5) years after termination. Standard exclusions apply (public, independently developed, already possessed).

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
6.1 Work Product. All deliverables created specifically for Client under this Agreement are work-made-for-hire and are Client's property upon payment in full. To the extent not treated as work-made-for-hire, Consultant assigns all right, title and interest in such deliverables to Client.
6.2 Consultant Tools. Consultant retains ownership of its pre-existing methodologies and templates, with a perpetual royalty-free license to Client for the extent necessary to use the deliverables.

7. NON-SOLICITATION
For twelve (12) months following termination, neither Party shall solicit or hire the other's employees or contractors, except for hires resulting from a general public advertisement not directed at such personnel.

8. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
Consultant warrants that (a) its engagement here does not breach any other agreement; (b) work will be performed in a professional, workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards; (c) deliverables will not infringe third-party IP.

9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Consultant's aggregate liability is capped at fees paid in the six (6) months preceding the claim. No consequential or lost-profit damages. Cap does not apply to Consultant's indemnification obligations or intentional misconduct.

10. GOVERNING LAW; DISPUTES
New York law. Disputes resolved by confidential binding arbitration under JAMS Comprehensive Rules in New York, NY.

11. GENERAL
Entire agreement; amendments in writing; no assignment without consent; notices to addresses on Schedule A; counterparts OK; severability.
