STATEMENT OF WORK #08

This Statement of Work ("SOW") is entered into as of March 4, 2026 ("Effective Date") and is governed by the Master Services Agreement dated October 1, 2025 between Demo Org, Inc. ("Provider") and Lumen Bio, Inc. ("Customer"). In the event of any conflict between this SOW and the MSA, the MSA shall control.

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
1.1 Name. "Project Helix — LIMS Modernization and Genomics Data Lake."
1.2 Duration. March 16, 2026 through May 22, 2026 (ten weeks).
1.3 Objective. Migrate Customer's lab-information-management system off legacy LabWare to a cloud-native architecture. Stand up a genomics data lake (Parquet on S3) for downstream R&D analytics.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider shall perform the following services:
(a) Discovery, current-state assessment, GxP impact analysis (Week 1–2);
(b) Cloud-native LIMS build (Week 2–6);
(c) Genomics data lake with parquet ingestion from 4 instrument types (Week 4–7);
(d) GxP/21 CFR Part 11 validation (Week 7–9);
(e) Parallel-run, cutover, and decommission planning (Week 10).
