STATEMENT OF WORK #07

This Statement of Work ("SOW") is entered into as of January 22, 2026 ("Effective Date") and is governed by the Master Services Agreement dated September 12, 2025 between Demo Org, Inc. ("Provider") and Caldera Health Networks ("Customer"). In the event of any conflict between this SOW and the MSA, the MSA shall control.

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
1.1 Name. "Project Vault — HIPAA-Compliant EHR Integration."
1.2 Duration. February 2, 2026 through April 17, 2026 (eleven weeks).
1.3 Objective. Integrate Customer's Epic EHR with three downstream analytics systems under a HIPAA-compliant data exchange. Replace the legacy nightly CSV drop with FHIR R4 streaming.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider shall perform the following services:
(a) HIPAA gap assessment and BAA review (Week 1);
(b) FHIR R4 connector build for Epic and three target systems (Week 2–6);
(c) PHI tokenization layer with audit trail (Week 4–7);
(d) Penetration testing and security review (Week 8–9);
(e) Pilot with two clinical workflows, then production cutover (Week 10–11).
