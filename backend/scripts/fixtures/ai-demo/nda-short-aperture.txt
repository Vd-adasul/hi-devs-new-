NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "NDA") is between Demo Org, Inc. ("Disclosing Party") and Aperture Science, Inc. ("Recipient"), effective February 14, 2026.

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
Recipient acknowledges that in connection with evaluating a potential commercial transaction, it will receive confidential information of Disclosing Party, including product roadmaps, internal pricing, and customer lists ("Confidential Information").

2. OBLIGATIONS
Recipient shall (a) use Confidential Information solely to evaluate the potential transaction; (b) protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, and in no event less than reasonable care; (c) disclose only to employees with a need to know who are bound by comparable confidentiality obligations; and (d) not reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any proprietary technology disclosed.

3. EXCLUSIONS
Confidential Information does not include information that (i) is publicly available, (ii) was rightfully in Recipient's possession, (iii) is independently developed without reference to Confidential Information, or (iv) is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction.

4. TERM
This NDA shall remain in effect for THREE (3) YEARS from the effective date. Confidentiality obligations survive for an additional two (2) years post-termination.

5. NO LICENSE
No license or other rights are granted, whether express or implied, under any intellectual property.

6. REMEDIES
Recipient acknowledges that monetary damages may be inadequate for breach and that Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief. The prevailing party shall be entitled to reasonable attorneys' fees.

7. GOVERNING LAW
Washington state law, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles. Exclusive jurisdiction in King County, Washington.

8. MISCELLANEOUS
This is a unilateral NDA — only Disclosing Party's information is protected. Entire agreement; amendments in writing; counterparts OK.
