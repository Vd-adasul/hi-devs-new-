STATEMENT OF WORK #04

This Statement of Work ("SOW") is entered into as of February 10, 2026 ("Effective Date") and is governed by the Master Services Agreement dated November 4, 2025 between Demo Org, Inc. ("Provider") and Vertex Cloud Holdings ("Customer"). In the event of any conflict between this SOW and the MSA, the MSA shall control, except with respect to the specific terms set forth herein.

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
1.1 Name. "Project Skywalk — Multi-Region Data Pipeline."
1.2 Duration. February 16, 2026 through April 10, 2026 (eight weeks).
1.3 Objective. Design and build a multi-region streaming data pipeline for Customer's telemetry platform; migrate legacy batch jobs; deliver a runbook for ongoing operations.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider shall perform the following services:
(a) Architecture and capacity-planning workshop (Week 1–2);
(b) Build streaming ingestion across two AWS regions (Week 2–5);
(c) Migrate twelve legacy batch jobs to the new pipeline (Week 4–6);
(d) Load test, failover test, and chaos validation (Week 6–7);
(e) Documentation, training, and handoff (Week 8).

3. MILESTONES AND DELIVERABLES

Milestone 1 — Architecture Approved              Due: Feb 27, 2026   Fee: $22,000
  Deliverables:
    - Reference architecture diagram (mermaid + draw.io)
    - Capacity model (RPS, GB/day, $/month projection)
    - Failover and disaster-recovery plan

Milestone 2 — Pipeline Live in Region 1          Due: Mar 13, 2026   Fee: $34,000
  Deliverables:
    - Ingestion service deployed to us-east-1
    - Schema registry populated with 14 event types
    - Observability dashboard (Datadog) with SLOs

Milestone 3 — Cross-Region + Legacy Migrated     Due: Mar 27, 2026   Fee: $42,000
  Deliverables:
    - Region 2 (us-west-2) live with auto-failover
    - All 12 batch jobs cut over to streaming
    - Backfill of trailing 30 days complete

Milestone 4 — Validation + Handoff               Due: Apr 10, 2026   Fee: $34,000
  Deliverables:
    - Load test report (10x peak traffic)
    - Chaos test report (region-out, broker-out, schema-evolution)
    - Operational runbook + 2 training sessions delivered

TOTAL FIXED FEE: $132,000 (exclusive of pre-approved AWS infrastructure pass-through)
